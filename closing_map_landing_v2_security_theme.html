The risk

Most buyers and sellers don't find out about problems until it's almost too late.

A lien surfaces two days before closing. An old judgment appears on the title. A boundary dispute nobody mentioned. These aren't rare exceptions. They happen in roughly one in three transactions.

When problems appear late, you lose leverage. You scramble. You pay more than you should to make it go away, or you walk away from a deal you wanted.

Closing Map exists because peace of mind should be at the beginning of your transaction, not just the end. Discover issues early, when you can still negotiate, plan, or walk away on your terms.
