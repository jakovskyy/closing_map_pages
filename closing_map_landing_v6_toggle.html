Most people find out the real numbers too late.

In NJ and NY real estate transactions, the final line-item costs surprise almost everyone. Closing costs, transfer taxes, lender fees, attorney fees — they add up in ways that rarely get explained until you are already in it. Closing Map puts those numbers in your hands before anything is signed.

The closing cost surprise is avoidable.

Between the down payment, closing costs, lender fees, and transfer taxes, buyers in NJ and NY often bring $15,000 to $30,000 more to closing than they planned for. The closing cost calculator shows you the full line-item breakdown before you are committed to anything. No agent conversation required first.

Most sellers don't know their net until closing day.

By then, there is nothing left to negotiate. Commission structures, transfer taxes, outstanding balances, and closing costs all reduce what you take home. The seller net sheet runs those numbers against your specific property before you commit to a listing price — so the number you see is the number you can plan around.
