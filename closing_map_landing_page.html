The problem

You're about to make the biggest purchase of your life. And nobody's giving you straight answers.

You piece together information from scattered Google searches, outdated blog posts, and friends who bought five years ago. You ask an agent and get a sales pitch. You ask a lender and get another sales pitch.

The result? Confusion, unrealistic expectations, and anxiety when you finally sit down to talk numbers. Not to mention that nagging feeling that someone's not telling you everything.

We built Closing Map because consumers deserve clarity before they're in someone's sales funnel. Learn first. Decide when you're ready.
